Meet the Authors

The Labor Wars was designed and written by John D'Agosta, Susan Weiner, Nat Budin, and Phoebe Roberts.

The universe in which the Labor Wars takes place was designed by John, Susan, Ian Schleifer and Erin Price and originally appeared in the LARP Counterculture, which ran at Intercon D.

Alleged Entertainment

Alleged Entertainment has been writing and producing LARPs since 2003. Founded at Brandeis University in Waltham, MA, Alleged Entertainment has created over a dozen original LARPs. Its 2008 "Time Travel Review Board" won top honors at the LARPA Small Games Contest.

Alleged Entertainment's home page is located at www.aegames.org.
